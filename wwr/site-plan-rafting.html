Rafting Site Plan

Alberto Gomez Arias

Overview

Purpose

To increase the revenue of the business in order aquire more transport vehicles, rafts, staff, and ultimately more clients.

Audience

The target customer is anyone who loves the outdoors and adventure. Any age group ranging from 5 to any. They are someone who likes to take on new challenges both big and small. They want information on white water rafting regarding difficulty, price, and length of time the trip will take. The site should be able to provide this information and allow for online pre registration and waiver signatures for shorter wait times at physical location.
