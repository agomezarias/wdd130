What we offer and Our Purpose

Our Purpose

Here at White Water adventure we strive to make your day a great day. By taking these trips along the Colorado river, we are able to both help our employees as well as Mother Nature. 20% of our proceeds go towards helping maintain the part of the river that we take people on. Our only regret is that we can't cover more of the river so that we could cover more maintenance square footage. We are grateful for the people we get to take out on our trips as it helps to educate persons about our great outdoors. We hope to be able to continue improving as time goes on and in the near future we are going to be taking bids in order to establish an animal sanctuary to help reabilitate injured or endangered animals.

What we offer

White Water Adventures offers tons of activities filled with thrills and joyful moments. We offer rafting for all kinds of groups whether you are an expert, or a beginner. Our trained guides are fit to take care of all of your needs so that you may focus on having a great adventure. Our packages are put together with folks of all walks of life.
